About Anne Frank

The Diary of Anne Frank is the powerful, real-life account of a young Jewish girl hiding from the Nazis during World War II. Written between 1942 and 1944 while Anne Frank and her family lived in a secret annex in Amsterdam, this diary captures her hopes, fears, and reflections with remarkable honesty and insight. Despite the horrors surrounding her, Anne’s words reveal a vibrant spirit and a deep longing for freedom and understanding. Her story remains a timeless testament to the resilience of the human spirit
